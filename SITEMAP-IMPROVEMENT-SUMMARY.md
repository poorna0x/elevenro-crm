# Sitemap SEO Improvements - Fixed #Section URLs & Enhanced Structure

## 🎯 Problem Solved
Fixed the major SEO issue where sitemap contained #section URLs (like `#services`, `#about`) which search engines treat as the same as the homepage, providing no SEO value.

## ✅ Improvements Implemented

### 1. **Removed #Section URLs** ✅
**Before:**
- `https://hydrogenro.com/#services`
- `https://hydrogenro.com/#about`
- `https://hydrogenro.com/#contact`
- `https://hydrogenro.com/#booking`

**After:**
- `https://hydrogenro.com/services`
- `https://hydrogenro.com/about`
- `https://hydrogenro.com/contact`
- `https://hydrogenro.com/booking`
- `https://hydrogenro.com/service-areas`
